Everything Your School Needs
In One Platform

From score capture to final report card generation, EDUNEXUS handles your entire exam workflow with precision and speed.

Score Capture
Subject teachers enter marks directly online per stream and exam. Bulk CSV upload available for fast data entry across all classes.
Published Results & Analytics
Auto-computed broadsheet with ranks, means, PLV levels, and grade distributions. Export to PDF with one click.
Report Cards
Generate individual and bulk report cards with teacher remarks, performance levels and scores formatted to print-ready standards.
Learner Registry
Manage complete student profiles including guardian contacts, class assignments, religion subjects, gender and full enrolment history.
Faculty Management
Assign teachers to classes and subjects. Control which teachers can access which streams with a precise role-based permission matrix.
Assessment Locks & Audit
Lock or unlock specific exam entry windows per class. Track submission status, reviews, and publishing across all streams in real time.
